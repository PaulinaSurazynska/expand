About me


I offer remote business development, marketing and account management services to small companies and startups.


I am dynamic, committed and outgoing person who constantly delivers results through bringing fresh thinking. My strong organisational abilities combined with interpersonal and communication skills enable me to work effectively with other people at all levels. I have extensive experience in sales, account management, customer service and marketing and constantly looking for new challenges and inspiring people who I will enjoy working with.

Services

Business Development

Developing and implementing growth opportunities

Services: business development strategy consultation, lead generation (cold calling, email campaigns), networking events, creating successful pitch, sales proposals, managing sales cycle.

Marketing

Communicating the value of your product, service or brand

Services: marketing strategy consultation, social media management, marketing materials creation, branding and identity, email marketing campaigns.

Account Management

Maintaining your existing relationships

Services: managing long term relationships with the portfolio of assigned clients, building understanding of the customer's demands, planing how to meet these demands, and generating sales as a result.
